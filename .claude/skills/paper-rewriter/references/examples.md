## Example 1: General Rewriting

**Intensity**: Medium
**Section Type**: Results

**Original:**
"The study shows that social media has a big impact on young people. Many students spend a lot of time on their phones. This is a problem for their education."

**Rewritten:**
"The investigation demonstrates that social media exerts a substantial influence on adolescents. Numerous students devote considerable time to mobile devices, which poses a significant challenge to their academic pursuits."

**Changes Made:**
- Sentence Structure: Combined sentences 2 and 3 using relative clause
- Vocabulary: shows‚Üídemonstrates, big‚Üísubstantial, impact‚Üíinfluence, young people‚Üíadolescents, many‚Üínumerous, spend‚Üídevote, a lot of‚Üíconsiderable, phones‚Üímobile devices, problem‚Üíchallenge, education‚Üíacademic pursuits
- Voice Changes: None needed

## Example 2: With Professional Terminology

**Intensity**: Light
**Section Type**: Methods

**Original:**
"We used ResNet-50 to train the model on ImageNet. The CNN achieved 95% accuracy. We used Adam optimizer with a learning rate of 0.001."

**Rewritten:**
"The model was trained on ImageNet using ResNet-50 architecture. The CNN attained an accuracy of 95%. Optimization was performed using Adam optimizer with a learning rate of 0.001."

**Changes Made:**
- Sentence Structure: Restructured sentence order, used passive voice
- Vocabulary: used‚Üíusing, achieved‚Üíattained, used‚Üíperformed using
- Preserved Terms: ResNet-50, ImageNet, CNN, Adam optimizer, learning rate
- Voice Changes: "We used" ‚Üí "was trained using" (active to passive)

**Key Point**: Notice that ResNet-50, ImageNet, CNN, Adam optimizer, and learning rate are all preserved exactly as written in the original.
