The watershed is an important unit for hydrological analysis. The hydrological cycle includes precipitation, evapotranspiration, infiltration, and runoff. The SCS curve number method is widely used to estimate direct runoff from rainfall [1]. The Green-Ampt model describes the infiltration process (Smith & Jones, 2019). The baseflow contribution to streamflow is significant during dry periods. The Manning's equation is used to calculate open channel flow velocity: V = (1/n)R^(2/3)S^(1/2) [2]. The hydrograph shows the variation of discharge over time at a specific point in the watershed.
